This week has seen unrest across the country with thousands of Americans calling for justice. We must not stay silent. Rioting is wrong, yes, but the murder of our black and brown people is unconscionable . In the 1993 International Chemical Weapons Convention tear gas is listed as a banned weapon, and yet they’re using it on our children, our priests, our neighbors. If a kind looking, elderly white man was abused trying to simply return a helmet and the police can watch him bleeding out on the ground and walk past, imagine what happens to young black men regularly in this country. Nothing justifies that abuse of power. This isn’t “their cause”. It’s America’s cause. The responsibility to change belongs to all of us. I believe we can get through this turbulent time, together. Its time to put the power into the hands of people who won't abuse it. The time to heal is now. Now, we dare to hope.
