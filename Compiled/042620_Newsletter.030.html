Friend,

                 

I hope this letter finds you and your family healthy and enjoying some spring weather (finally!). It has been another busy week for our campaign and I want to share an update with what our team has been up to.

On Monday, I was proud to announce being endorsed by US Senator Kirsten Gilibrand.


Earth Day

On Wednesday, we celebrated the 50th Anniversary of Earth Day across the District. I am proud of my history fighting polluters like Tonawanda Coke and being endorsed by the Sierra Club. I am also proud to live in New York which has enacted the most progressive climate change legislation in the nation, while Trump rolls back regulations to keep our air and water clean. If you missed honoring Earth Day this week, please check out the celebration here.
Just outside the District, Devil’s Hole in the Niagara Gorge is one of my favorite hikes- check it out!

Election Update

The Governor announced this week that NYS will automatically mail all registered voters postage-paid applications for an absentee ballot. More details to come on when those applications will be mailed. Remember, the Special Election is the same day as New York State Primary Day on June 23rd - tell your friends!

COVID-19 Update

We are flattening the curve! Adhering to social distancing is not easy, do you or need family need help? Please contact us if you are need of support. Our team made nearly 50,000 phone calls to the district to check in and make sure people can access the resources they need to navigate this disaster. We are happy to help!


Campaign Update

Thanks to amazing supporters like you, our campaign outraised Chris Jacobs in the final quarterly FEC financial report before the Special Election. Nate received 15x the number of individual donations than Jacobs and the majority of these donations were in District. Watch Nate here discussing how our grassroots campaign can win.


Join Us!

With less than 2 months to go, your grassroots support is critical to sending Nate to Congress! We are looking for hosts for our digital house parties to help us reach every voter in the district in the coming weeks. If you or someone you know are interested in organizing a private virtual party with Nate, please fill out this form and we'll get in touch.

If you're interested in making calls from home, please click here to get started or email Volunteers@votemcmurray.com for more information.

After another week of Trump’s blame, aggression, and scapegoating, many of us are left exhausted. Please don’t despair. This past week I reflected on the words of a former Upstate New York leader, Robert Kennedy, and it brought me so much hope. I hope it does the same for you.

“Each time a man stands up for an ideal, or acts to improve the lot of others, or strikes out against injustice, he sends forth a tiny ripple of hope, and crossing each other from a million different centers of energy and daring those ripples build a current which can sweep down the mightiest walls of oppression and resistance.”

Stay well,
Nathan McMurray

Endorsed Candidate for U.S. Congress
